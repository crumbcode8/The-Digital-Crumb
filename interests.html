Hobbies & Interests

My interests are a big part of how I stay inspired and grounded. They give me space to explore creativity, pay attention to detail, and bring a sense of care into everything I make.

I enjoy baking, especially creating playful, thoughtful desserts that feel like small celebrations. I also love visual art and design—painting, photography, and experimenting with color and texture—which naturally influences how I think about interfaces and layouts.

Time with my children, exploring local spots, and noticing the small details in everyday life all feed into my work. I’m inspired by environments that feel warm, imaginative, and intentional, and I aim to bring that same feeling into my digital projects.
